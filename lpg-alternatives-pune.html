🧭 Quick Decision Guide
🏢 Housing Society / Large Building
Era Hydrobiotech (BARC Nisarga-Runa) or MNGL PNG pipeline — both handle bulk scale. PNG is simplest; biogas is self-sustaining with wet waste.
🏠 Single Apartment / Flat
Vaayu Mitra (balcony/terrace unit) or MNGL PNG (if pipeline exists in your building). Cheapest long-term option.
🍽️ Restaurant / Cloud Kitchen
Vaayu Mitra's 10 kg/day unit (₹1.1L) or MNGL PNG commercial connection. Saves ₹40,000–₹80,000/year vs LPG at commercial rates.
🌾 Farm / Bungalow with Garden
HomeBiogas (outdoor backyard unit, uses food waste + animal manure) or B-Sustain modular plant. Bonus: liquid bio-fertilizer for crops.
